01 — About Us

A small room with good light, better people, and the smell of coffee.

Molies Kafe began as a shared idea between three friends who wanted a slower kind of café — somewhere you could read for an hour without anyone asking if you'd like the bill. We make our coffee carefully, bake most things in-house, and try to know our regulars by name.

We open early, close before dark, and keep the music quiet. There is always a window seat.

i.
Single-origin beans, roasted weekly within 30km.
ii.
Kitchen open till close. No microwaves.
iii.
Wi-Fi exists. You'll have to ask for it.
02 — Services

What we offer.

01

Dine In

A seat, a cup, and no rush. Come sit with us on weekends — we keep the music quiet and the coffee careful.

02

Takeaway

Order at the counter and carry it out. Everything travels well — croissants, nasi lemak, and your flat white.

03

Delivery

Can't make it in? We deliver via Grab and other platforms on weekends. Same food, same care, at your door.
