01 — About Us

A small café, a tent, and a kitchen that never stops tasting.

Molie's Kafe is a small, laid-back café where the tables sit under an open tent and the counter is always within arm's reach. A handful of seats, a neighbourhood rhythm, and the kind of welcome that makes you want to stay for a second slice.

Every cake on our counter comes from a local warm kitchen. The brews are classic, the flavours are honest, and the door is always open for the unhurried.
