01 — About Us

A small room with good light, better people, and the smell of coffee.

Molies Kafe began as a shared idea between three friends who wanted a slower kind of café — somewhere you could read for an hour without anyone asking if you'd like the bill. We make our coffee carefully, bake most things in-house, and try to know our regulars by name.

We open early, close before dark, and keep the music quiet. There is always a window seat.

i.
Single-origin beans, roasted weekly within 30km.
ii.
Kitchen open till close. No microwaves.
iii.
Wi-Fi exists. You'll have to ask for it.
02 — History

How we got here.

2018

One espresso machine, one stubborn idea.

Molies opened in a 30sqm shoplot off SS2, with secondhand stools and a hand-painted menu board. We sold out of pastries on day three.

2020

The lockdown loaves.

When the doors closed we baked sourdough from home and delivered by bicycle. Most of those customers still come in every week.

2022

A bigger room. Same rules.

We moved next door, doubled the seating, and kept the original counter — chips, scratches, and all. The window seat survived intact.

Today

Still figuring it out.

Eight years in, we're still tweaking the espresso ratio and arguing about the croissant recipe. Come watch us work.
